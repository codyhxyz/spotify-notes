### Privacy Policy 

#### Introduction

Welcome to my-spotify-notes.vercel.app, a platform crafted with the utmost respect for your privacy. We are committed to being transparent and sincere in the collection and use of your data. This policy provides details on the practices and policies regarding data collection, use, and sharing applied to our Spotify Developer Application (SDA). Please read it thoroughly to understand your rights and options regarding your data.

#### Data Collection

We adopt a minimalistic approach to data collection to ensure the essential functionality and security of the service:

- **Information you voluntarily provide:** When using our app, all notes that you create are stored securely in a Supabase database.
- **Automatically collected data:** To facilitate account creation and authentication, we store the email address associated with your Spotify account.

#### Data Use

We prioritize your privacy in the utilization of the data collected:

- **Functionality and security:** The collected data assists in providing a personalized experience and ensuring the security of your account.
- **Third-party sharing:** We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. 

#### Cookies

To enhance your user experience, we may utilize cookies with the following stipulations:

- **Use of Cookies:** We use cookies primarily to maintain your login status, facilitating a seamless user experience.
- **Third-party Cookies:** We do not permit third parties to place cookies on your browser to collect information about your browsing activities.
- **Cookie Management:** While cookies aid in enhancing your user experience, you retain full control over the management of cookies through various tools. Be advised that blocking cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

#### Contact Us

For inquiries, clarifications, or concerns regarding your data or this privacy policy, please feel free to reach out to us at beangreenes ('at') yahoo ('dot') com.

#### Updates to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy periodically to reflect any changes in our practices. It is advisable to review this policy frequently to stay updated. The date at the end of this policy indicates the last update.
