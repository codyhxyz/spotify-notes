# Privacy Policy 

## Introduction

We collect only the data necessary for the functionality you expect from the service, my-spotify-notes.vercel.app. This app is super minimal and exists mainly to satisfy my needs as its creator, while also sharing it with the public for free so that others can enjoy it as well. I am not interested in making money off this endeavour or collecting any unnecessary information, or selling your information for the sake of profit.

## Data Collection

- **Information you provide:** All notes written on this app are stored securely in a database via Supabase.
- **Automatically collected data:**  We store your Spotify email address to associate an account with you for our authentication purposes.

## Data Use
We do not share your information with any third parties.

## Cookies
* We may use cookies to keep you logged in for your convenience
* We do not allow third parties to place cookies on your browser for any reason, including to track browsing activities.
* You are free to block cookies at any time using tools such as uBlock Origin in which case we cannot be held liable for loss of functionality from the website.
